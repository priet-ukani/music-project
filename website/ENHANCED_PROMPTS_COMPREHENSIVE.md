### **TELANGANA** (MANDATORY - PRIMARY FOCUS)
**Traditional/Folk:**
1. **Darshanam Mogilaiah** (Kinnera instrument master)

   - Instrument: Kinnera (ancient string instrument)
   - Awards: Padma Shri 2022
   - Genre: Tribal folk, devotional
   - Notable: Revived nearly extinct Kinnera tradition
   - Active: 1940s-present

2. **Gaddam Sammayya** (Oggu Katha performer)
   - Community: Kuruma (shepherd) community
   - Genre: Oggu Katha (narrative ballad tradition)
   - Specialty: Epic storytelling with dappu drum
   - Region: Warangal district
   - Active: 1960s-2010s

3. **Goreti Venkanna** (Folk singer - "Janapadala Hrudaya Samrat")
   - Genre: Janapada Geethalu (folk songs)

   - Language: Telugu folk
   - Active: 1980s-present
   - Awards: Multiple state honors

4. **Chintha Ravi Shankar Sharma** (Burra Katha artist)
   - Genre: Burra Katha (traditional storytelling with tambura)
   - Community: Traditional bard community
   - Specialty: Social commentary through narrative
   - Active: 1970s-present

5. **Vimalakka (Ananthula Vimala)** (Revolutionary folk singer)
   - Genre: Political folk songs, protest music
   - Notable: Songs for Telangana statehood movement
   - Style: Contemporary folk with traditional instruments
   - Active: 1990s-present

6. **Kinnera Mogulaiah Family Ensemble**
